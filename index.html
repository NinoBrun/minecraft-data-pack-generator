Data Pack File Structure Generator for Minecraft

Only alphanumeric characters, hyphens, and underscores are allowed
Double quotes and backslashes are not allowed
• Find and open your .minecraft folder on your PC
• Go to the "saves" folder
• Open the folder of the world you want to add the data pack to
• Go to the "datapacks" folder
• Place your data pack in the "datapacks" folder
• If you are in the world already, type /reload in the chat to load the data pack
• Download VSCode
• Go to the extensions and download "Datapack Helper Plus" by Spyglass
